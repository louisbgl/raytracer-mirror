Usage: raytracer <input_file>

Currently supported:
Shapes:
	Plane (position (x, y, z), normal (nx, ny, nz), material)
	Sphere (position (x, y, z), radius, material)
	Cylinder (position (x, y, z), radius, material)
	LimitedCylinder (position (x, y, z), radius, height, material)
	Rectangle (position (x, y, z), width, height, material)
	Box (position (x, y, z), width, height, depth, orientation, material)
	Torus (position (x, y, z), majorRadius, minorRadius, material)
	Tanglecube (position (x, y, z), scale, material)
    Cone (position (x, y, z), radius, material)
    LimitedCone (position (x, y, z), radius, height, material)
    Hourglass (position (x, y, z), radius, material)
    Limited Hourglass (position (x, y, z), radius, height, material)
Materials:
	Lambertian (name, color (r, g, b))
	Transparent (name, opacity, refractiveIndex, color (r, g, b))
	ColoredDiffuse (name, color (r, g, b))
	Phong (name, color (r, g, b), shininess)
Lights:
	PointLight (position (x, y, z), color (r, g, b), intensity)
	DirectionalLight (direction (dx, dy, dz), color (r, g, b), intensity)
